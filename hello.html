Here's a snippet of my upcoming book!

A modern Fantasy/Adventure story based in the near future. Set in what is now known as Central Mexico, we focus on 3 Magers that venture off to complete their Pilgrimage. Visiting all 14 temples to prove proficiency in each respective element a given temple is based on.

Facets in this novel include:

  1. An advanced society that utilizes crystal technology to manipulate certain elements.
  2. A post societal collapse culture.
  3. Unique technology, flora, fauna, elements (magic) & creatures.

Although this novel is for all ages, there are difficult or adult concepts that do take place. Such as:

  • Violence (combat, battles, skirmishes) & death.
  • Beings from different dimensions.
  • Economic, societal & cultural instability.
